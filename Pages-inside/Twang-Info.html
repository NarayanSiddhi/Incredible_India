Description

Twang is a city located in the Tawang district of the northeastern Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh. Nestled in the Himalayas, Twang is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, including snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and lush green landscapes. The city is home to the famous Tawang Monastery, one of the largest monasteries in the world, which holds significant religious and cultural importance for Buddhists. Visitors to Twang can experience a unique blend of Tibetan culture, explore ancient monastic traditions, and enjoy the tranquility of its picturesque surroundings.
