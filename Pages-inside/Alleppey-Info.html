Description

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is a picturesque town in the southern Indian state of Kerala. Renowned for its serene backwaters, Alleppey offers a unique experience of houseboat cruises through a network of canals, lakes, and lagoons. The town is adorned with lush paddy fields, coconut groves, and intricate waterways, creating a tranquil and scenic atmosphere. Alleppey is famous for its annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race, a traditional event showcasing beautifully decorated snake boats. Additionally, visitors can explore the vibrant Alleppey Beach, historic architecture, and savor the authentic flavors of Kerala cuisine, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat.
